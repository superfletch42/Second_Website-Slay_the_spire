What is slay the spire?

Slay the spire is a rougelike card battler with a diverse set of cards and four unique characters. The game features many tough opponents, mini-bosses and big bosses making for challenging and unique gameplay every run. You ascend a tower and explore a map, choosing paths that offer fights, random events, mini-bosses or an oppurtunity to heal.

What do I like about it?

  • I like how different and unique each characters card set and gameplay are.
  • Finding strong builds and unique combos to create a strong synergy.
  • How many unique relics there is to find to enchance the many cards.
  • The amount of unique and different enemy types there is to fight and beat.
